Payment Process

  1. Select your desired deposit amount (minimum ₹1,000)
  2. System displays the official wallet address and supported network
  3. Send cryptocurrency from your wallet to the displayed address
  4. Upload screenshot of your transaction for faster verification
  5. Transaction is verified on the blockchain (may take a few minutes)
  6. Balance and applicable bonus are credited after confirmation
Transaction Status Updates: While Verifying: Your transaction is being verified on the blockchain. This may take a few minutes depending on network traffic. After Success: Deposit successful. Your balance has been updated.
